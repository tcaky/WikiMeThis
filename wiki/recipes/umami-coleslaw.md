# Umami Coleslaw 

## Ingredients
- ½ Cabbage
- 2 tbsp Sesame oil
- 1 tbsp Soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
- ½ tsp Garlic paste
- ½ tsp Salt

## Directions 

1. Mix everything except cabbage. 
2. Slice cabbage to desired size. Microwave for 2-3 minutes to wilt cabbage. 
3. Mix cabbage and dressing together. 
4. Let sit 10 minutes. 
5. Mix one more time and serve.
